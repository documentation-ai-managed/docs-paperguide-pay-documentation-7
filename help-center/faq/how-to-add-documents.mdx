## Overview

To add a new document to Paperguide-pay, use the dashboard.

## Steps

1. Log in to your Paperguide-pay account
2. Select your project
3. Click **Add Document**
4. Upload your file (PDF, DOCX, or TXT)
5. Enter a title and description
6. Click **Save**

Documents are processed automatically and appear in your project list.
