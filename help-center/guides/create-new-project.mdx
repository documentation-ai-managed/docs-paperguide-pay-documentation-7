## Overview

Start organizing documents by creating a new project.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access dashboard">
    Log in at `https://dashboard.paperguide-pay.com`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create project">
    Click **New Project**, enter a name, and click **Create**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add documents">
    Use the **Add Document** button to upload files
  </Step>
</Steps>
